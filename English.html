English



i) How



For example-
How are you doing?,

How is the weather today?,

How can I access Wi-Fi or mobile data in this area?



ii) What



For example-
What are the must-see attractions here?,

What's the local currency, and where can I exchange money?



iii) Where



For example-
Where can I find a good restaurant or cafe nearby?,

Where is the nearest (or best) hotel?,
Where can I find public restrooms or bathrooms?,

Where is the nearest pharmacy/hospital?



iv) Are



For example-
Are there any guided tours available?,

Are there any emergency numbers I should be aware of?



v) Is



For example-
Is everything okay?,

Is he/she going to take us to our destination?,

Is it safe here at night?



vi) Can



For example-
Can we go to (the destination) at night?,

Can we get medicines/food/accessories delivered here?



Words and some sentences used for greeting:



Words:


i)Hello,

ii)Hi,

iii)Hey,

iv)Good morning,

v)Good afternoon,

vi) Good evening,

vii)Good night



Sentences:


i) You are looking gorgeous/pretty/handsome/very nice/cute



How to show appreciation:


i)Thank you so much!,

ii)You are so kind,

iii)I appreciate you for (helping me/doing that for me),

iv)You are such a lovely person,

v)You have made me very happy,

vi)You have been a great help,

vii)Thank God you were there to help me



How to show dissatisfaction:


i)That is/was not nice,

ii) You/He/She/They are rude,

iii)I did/do not like it,

iv)I don't want to go there,

v)That was disappointing,

vi)I felt hurt,

vii)That was saddening



How to say you don't feel good:


i)I do not feel good,

ii) My (particular part of the body) is aching

iii)I have an upset stomach,

iv)I feel suffocated,

v)I am having an allergy attack



How to say you feel threatened:


i)I am scared/afraid,

ii)I feel threatened,

iii)Can we go somewhere safer?
