Hindi



i) How
i) Kaisa/Kaise/Kaisi



For example-
How are you doing?, Aap KAISE/KAISI hain?

How is the weather today?, Aaj ka mausam KAISA hain?

How can I access Wi-Fi or mobile data in this area?, Main is kshetra mein Wi-Fi ya mobile data KAISE upayog kar sakta hoon?


ii) What
ii) Kya



For example-
What are the must-see attractions here?, Yahaan avashy dekhane laayak aakarshan KYA hain?

What's the local currency, and where can I exchange money?, Yahaan ka sthaaneey paisa KYA hai aur main paise ka aadaan-pradaan kahaan kar sakta hoon?



iii) Where
iii) Kahaan



For example-
Where can I find a good restaurant or cafe nearby?, - Mujhe aas-paas koi achchha restaurant ya cafe KAHAAN mil sakata hain?

Where is the nearest (or best) hotel?, Yahaan sabse kareebi (ya phir sabse achchha) hotel KAHAAN hain?

Where can I find public restrooms or bathrooms?,Mujhe saarvajanik shauchaalay ya bathroom KAHAAN mil sakta hain?

Where is the nearest pharmacy/hospital?, Sabse kareebi dawaai-ki-dukaan/hospital KAHAAN pe hain?


The words "Are", "Is" and "Can" cannot be direclty translated to Hindi. The questions that are asked using these words in English can be asked in Hindi language using the words mentioned in the list above.



iv) Are
iv) The common questions using the word "Are" can be asked in the following way:


For example-
Are there any guided tours available?, Kya yahan koi "guided-tours" hote hain?

Are there any emergency numbers I should be aware of?, Kya yahan koi aapaatakaal numberain hain jinke baarein mein mujhe pata hona chahiye?



v) Is
v) The common questions using the word "Is" can be asked in the following way:


For example-
Is everything okay?, Kya sabkuch theek hain?

Is he/she going to take us to our destination?, Kya woh hammein hamaari manzil tak pohuchayenge?

Is it safe here at night?, Kya ye jagah raat mein surakshit hai?


vi) Can
vi) The common questions using the word "Can" can be asked in the following way:


For example-
Can we go to (the destination) at night?, Kya hum raat ko (the desired destination) ja sakte hain?

Can we get medicines/food/accessories delivered here?, Kya yahaan dawaai/khaana/saamaan mangwaya ja sakta hain?


Words and some sentences used for greeting:
Abhivaadan ke liye vyavahar kiye huwe shabd aur vaakya :



Words:
Shabd:


i)Hello, Namaste/Namashkar

ii)Hi, Namaste/Namashkar

iii)Hey, Namaste/Namashkar

iv)Good morning, Shubh Prabhat

v)Good afternoon, Shubh Dupeher

vi) Good evening, Shubh Sandhya

vii)Good night, Shubh raatri



Sentences:
Vaakya:


i) You are looking gorgeous/pretty/handsome/very nice/cute, Aap badhe sundar/achhe/khoobsurat lag rahein hain.


How to show appreciation:
Saraahana kaise pradarshit karein:


i)Thank you so much!, Bohot dhanyavaad

ii)You are so kind, Aap badhe achchhe hain

iii)I appreciate you for (helping me/doing that for me), Meri sahayata karne ke liye shukriyaan

iv)You are such a lovely person, Aap badhe pyare insaan hain

v)You have made me very happy, Aapne mujhko badha khush kar diya

vi)You have been a great help, Aapne hamari bohot madat ki hain

vii)Thank God you were there to help me, Bhagwan Ka shukr hai ki aapne meri madat ki.



How to show dissatisfaction:
Asantosh kaise dikhayein:


i)That is/was not nice, Woh achchha nahi tha

ii)You/He/She/They are rude, Woh badhe ashisht hain

iii)I did/do not like it, Hummein woh pasand nahi aaya

iv)I don't want to go there, Hummein waha nahi jaana

v)That was disappointing, Woh niraashaajanak tha

vi)I felt hurt, Hummein bohot bura laga

vii)That was saddening, Bohot kasht huwa



How to say you don't feel good:
Aap kaisein bataayein aapka tabiyat kharab hain:


i)I do not feel good, Hummein achha nahi lag raha hai

ii) My (particular part of the body) is aching, Mera (shareer ka koi bhaag) dukh raha hai

iii)I have an upset stomach, Mere pet mein dard ho raha hai

iv)I feel suffocated, Hummein ghutan ho rahi hain

v)I am having an allergy attack, Mujhe ek allergy attack ho raha hai



How to say you feel threatened:
Kaise kahen ki aapako khatara mahasoos ho raha hai


i)I am scared/afraid, Hummein darr lag raha hai

ii)I feel threatened, Mujhe khatra mehsus ho raha hai

iii)Can we go somewhere safer?, Kya hum koi aur zyada surakhshit jagah jaa sakte hain?
